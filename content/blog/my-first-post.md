# Welcome to My Blog

I'm excited to launch this blog as I embark on my PhD journey in Legal AI. This space will serve as a platform to share my research insights, thoughts on AI/ML, and reflections on the intersection of artificial intelligence and socio-technical systems.

## Why Legal AI?

The legal domain presents unique challenges for AI systems:

- **Complex reasoning**: Legal arguments require multi-step logical reasoning
- **Interpretability**: Legal decisions must be explainable and transparent
- **Context-dependent**: Laws and their interpretations vary by jurisdiction and context
- **Ethical considerations**: AI systems must align with legal and ethical principles

## My Research Focus

My PhD will explore **neuro-symbolic architectures** for legal reasoning. I'm particularly interested in:

1. **Hybrid AI systems** that combine neural networks with symbolic reasoning
2. **Explainable AI** for legal decision-making
3. **Knowledge representation** for legal concepts and relationships
4. **Reasoning over legal texts** and case law
