# Starting My PhD Journey in Legal AI

I've just begun my PhD in Legal AI (January 2026), transitioning from my role as a Senior ML Engineer at CVS Health...

## My Research Focus

My PhD explores **socio-technical approaches to Legal AI**, combining:

1. **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)** for representing legal knowledge and case relationships
2. **Large Language Models (LLMs)** for understanding and generating legal text
3. **Neuro-symbolic architectures** that blend neural and symbolic reasoning
4. **Socio-technical perspectives** examining AI's impact on legal practice and society

## Why Socio-Technical?

Legal AI isn't just a technical challenge—it's deeply intertwined with:
- Human decision-making processes
- Social justice and equity
- Professional practices in law
- Ethical and societal implications
